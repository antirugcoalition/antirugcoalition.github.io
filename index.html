What We Stand For

Rug pulls and exit scams have damaged the reputation of blockchain innovation. The AntiRug Coalition is a collective of developers, investors, and advocates committed to promoting transparency and protecting communities from fraud.

Every project listed here has passed community review and adheres to our open standards for safety and accountability.

The AntiRug Standards

Locked Liquidity

Funds are locked or vested to ensure stability and prevent sudden withdrawals.

Open Source Code

Contracts are publicly verifiable and reviewed by independent contributors.

Transparent Teams

Developers are verified or community-trusted, ensuring accountability and open governance.
